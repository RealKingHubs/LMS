How the calendar works

Each semester balances learning and hands-on practice.

Month 1 Core learning block with guided lessons and weekly tasks.
Month 2 Applied learning with projects, labs, and instructor support.
Month 3 Production practice with portfolio, systems, or deployment focus.
Month 4 Hands-on lab month with guided build work, testing, and final showcase delivery.
